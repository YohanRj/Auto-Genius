Revolutionizing Vehicle Management through Intelligent Vehicle Assistance

In a world where vehicle ownership is prevalent yet expertise in car maintenance remains a challenge, a team of innovators sought to revolutionize the way drivers navigate car-related hurdles. Recognizing the common struggles from breakdowns to insurance complexities, they envisioned a solution: a cutting-edge mobile application designed to empower users with comprehensive vehicle assistance. This app, fueled by advanced technologies like sentiment analysis and neural networks, aims to offer a personalized guide for maintenance, spare parts, and insurance, redefining the way we handle car troubles.

  • Vehicle maintenance
  • Price prediction for spare part replacement
  • Suggestion of best insurance policy company

Research Gap

01

Revolutionizing Breakdown Assistance: Personalized and Real-Time Solutions

Existing breakdown systems often fall short in offering tailored and precise assistance, lacking specificity and real-time insights crucial for accurate recommendations. These systems typically provide generic advice, overlooking crucial details like vehicle specifications and real-time service station data, missing out on opportunities to deliver personalized solutions for drivers in need.

02

Personalized Predictive Maintenance System

Present vehicle maintenance systems lack the crucial elements of personalization and accuracy, offering generic advice that overlooks the specific needs of individual vehicles. A significant research void exists in developing a predictive maintenance system utilizing real-time service station data to deliver personalized tips based on a vehicle's make, model, and year. By integrating machine learning algorithms, this system aims to revolutionize maintenance practices, enriching owners with tailored advice to optimize upkeep, cut costs, and avert unforeseen breakdowns.

03

Advanced Damage Assessment

The research explores advanced technologies like computer vision and predictive maintenance for vehicle diagnostics. However, there's a need to bridge these innovations into a user-friendly app, integrating damage assessment, predictive maintenance, and spare part pricing seamlessly. Enhancing the insurance guidance feature with real-time data analysis could offer more personalized recommendations, filling a gap in informed decision-making for user.

04

Tailored Insurance Prediction App

A cutting-edge mobile app that revolutionizes insurance selection by leveraging vehicle owner reviews. Through sophisticated data analysis, the app automatically predicts the top insurance company tailored to individual user needs. Exclusive access to this predictive feature ensures precision in insurance choices, while the app generates comprehensive reports showcasing the top-ranked insurance provider, available for easy viewing within the mobile interface.

Research Problem

The automotive industry has seen a significant transition recently, with vehicles growing more complex as cutting-edge technologies are integrated into them. The entire user experience, efficiency, and safety of vehicles have all significantly improved as a result of this shift. However, there are difficulties in properly controlling and maintaining modern vehicles as a result of their complexity. Traditional vehicle management systems frequently rely on manual processes and sparse data consumption, which has a negative impact on performance, raises maintenance costs, and prevents them from utilizing emerging technologies to their fullest extent. The goal of this study's central research question is to investigate and recommend methods for radically altering vehicle management procedures through the use of intelligent vehicle support systems. To develop a new paradigm for vehicle management, these systems combine the capabilities of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, and real-time communication. By providing proactive and individualized support to both vehicle owners and service providers, the major objective is to improve user satisfaction while enhancing vehicle performance, increasing operational efficiency, lowering maintenance costs, and reducing downtime


Research objectives

Research objectives

Vehicle breakdown

    provide a holistic solution for vehicle management that helps drivers maintain and repair their vehicles with ease and confidence, while also offering valuable insights and information to prevent unexpected issues.

  • Sub Objectives
  • Offer repair suggestions and guidance to users who input their vehicle issue.
  • Connect users with nearby service stations or mechanics if the issue cannot be fixed by the user.
  • Provide drivers with a convenient and self-sufficient solution for repairing and learning about their vehicles.

Vehicle maintenance

    This solution provides personalized maintenance tips based on each vehicle's specifics, reducing breakdowns and costs. It enhances owners' knowledge for better upkeep, ensuring a smoother, worry-free experience on the road.

  • Sub Objectives
  • Create an intuitive mobile app interface enabling users to input vehicle details—model, make, and year—for personalized maintenance tips.
  • Engineer a robust machine learning algorithm capable of analyzing service station data to generate tailored maintenance tips for each user's specific vehicle.
  • Seamlessly integrate service station data into the system, ensuring the accuracy and relevance of maintenance tips and information provided to users.

Damage assessment

    The research aims to create a multifunctional mobile app empowering vehicle owners. It focuses on automating damage assessment using cutting-edge tech like computer vision, offering predictive maintenance, fair spare part pricing, and aiding insurance selection. Ultimately, it aims to provide a user-friendly tool for efficient vehicle maintenance and informed decision-making.

  • Sub Objectives
  • Use CNNs to accurately identify vehicle damage from uploaded images.
  • Provide reliable spare part prices using Time Series predictions from historical data.
  • Offer advice on choosing the best insurance based on coverage and user reviews.
  • Ensure an intuitive interface for easy navigation and accessibility.



Suggestion of best insurance policy company

    The main goal of a machine learning-based system is to recommendation of best insurance policy company according to the ratings.

  • Sub Objectives
  • Retrieve the rating of insurance policy companies
  • Analyze the rating of insurance policy companies
  • Suggest the best insurance policy company to the user
  • Find correct employer duration from insurance company
  • Maintain and update data of insurance policy companies
  • In the insurance firm, locate the appropriate dataset for various situations

Methodology

Divided into key components, it begins with a personal assistant aiding users in understanding dashboard indicators. By employing a CNN model integrated into a chatbot, users can upload indicator images for quick diagnostics. The second part focuses on vehicle damage assessment using InceptionV3 for severity analysis and LSTM for spare parts price prediction. The third component emphasizes predictive maintenance through machine learning algorithms, estimating upcoming procedures based on mileage and vehicle details. Lastly, it suggests the best insurance by analyzing user experiences and correlations between insurance and vehicle data, employing machine learning techniques for decision-making.
